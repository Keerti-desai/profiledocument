Clone of nykaa.com

Nykaa is an Indian e-commerce company, founded by Falguni Nayar in 2012 and headquartered in Mumbai. It sells beauty, wellness and fashion products across websites, mobile apps and 100+ offline stores. In 2020, it became the first Indian unicorn startup headed by a woman.[4]

In this project i have tried to make a look alike clone of Nykaa. With my efforts and the technology stack, that i have learned so far in the Edureka internship.
